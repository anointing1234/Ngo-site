Who We Are

Promoting Social and Cultural Support for Women & Children

The Akajiugo Charity Foundation is a social-cultural, non-governmental organization dedicated to supporting, uplifting, and protecting women and children through cultural advocacy, welfare programs, emotional care, and community-driven initiatives.

We work to preserve our cultural values while ensuring vulnerable families receive the care, support, and protection they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide emotional, cultural, and social support for women and children—ensuring safety, empowerment, and dignity in our communities.

Our Vision

A community where women and children thrive, are protected, and have access to cultural, social, and emotional resources for a better future.

Our Completed Community Support & Cultural Preservation Projects

Women Welfare & Relief Outreach

Over 1,000 vulnerable women supported with food supplies, health items, and emotional care during community outreach programs.

Child Care & Educational Support

Providing school materials, holiday programs, and safe-space activities for children in underserved communities.

Cultural Heritage Preservation

Organizing festivals, awareness campaigns, and cultural education programs to preserve our traditions and identity.

Women Health & Community Awareness

Offering free health education, sanitary kits, counseling services, and sensitization programs for women across multiple communities.
