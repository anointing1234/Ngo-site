Our Mission: Supporting Women & Protecting Children

The **Akajiugo Charity Foundation** is committed to providing compassionate support and intervention for **women, widows, vulnerable children, and underserved families** across Imo State. Our mission is to uplift communities through **caregiving programs, social-cultural development, child welfare initiatives, emotional support, empowerment aid, and sustainable community outreach**.

  • Providing food, health assistance, and relief packages for widows and single mothers.
  • Ensuring child safety, education support, and welfare for vulnerable and at-risk children.
  • Preserving cultural identity through community engagement and social-cultural activities.
  • Offering emotional support, counseling, and care to women facing difficult circumstances.
  • Promoting community unity and welfare through consistent outreach programs.

We exist to restore dignity, strengthen families, and give women and children the opportunity to thrive regardless of their situation.

Our Vision: A Safe & Supportive Future for Every Woman & Child

Our vision is a society where **no woman is left without support** and **no child is left unprotected**. We aim to become one of the leading humanitarian foundations in Imo State—recognized for advocacy, care, cultural preservation, and life-changing outreach.

  • A future where widows and single mothers receive consistent care and dignity.
  • A community where every child has access to food, education, and emotional support.
  • Strengthening family structures through cultural and social programs.
  • Expanding outreach to rural areas across all LGAs in Imo State.
  • Building sustainable partnerships for long-term humanitarian impact.

Our History: Built on Compassion & Community

The **Akajiugo Charity Foundation** was founded in **Imo State** as a response to the growing needs of disadvantaged women, widows, and vulnerable children in local communities.

What started as small neighborhood outreach—offering food, emotional support, and cultural assistance—has grown into a structured organization providing organized community care, child welfare activities, and women support initiatives.

Today, the foundation continues to expand, touching lives across Imo State with a deep commitment to compassion, dignity, and community upliftment.
