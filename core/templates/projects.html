Success Stories: Projects Supporting Women & Children

Vocational Skills Training for Women

Over 300 women trained in tailoring, baking, and handicrafts, gaining financial independence.

Child Education Program

Providing access to education, learning materials, and mentorship for over 500 children.

Health & Wellness Initiatives

Medical outreach and wellness programs benefitting over 400 mothers and children.

Community Development & Empowerment

Organized workshops, cultural programs, and support initiatives improving the welfare of families.

Akajiugo Charity Foundation is a social-cultural non-governmental organisation dedicated to the support, protection, and welfare of **women and children in Imo State**. We promote care, advocacy, cultural heritage, and community wellbeing.
