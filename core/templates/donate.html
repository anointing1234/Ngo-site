Support Our Mission

Help Us Care for Women & Children in Imo State

Your contribution enables the Akajiugo Charity Foundation to provide essential support, education, and welfare programs for vulnerable women and children. Donations help fund vocational training, healthcare initiatives, educational materials, and community empowerment projects that uplift entire families. Every donation, big or small, brings hope and a brighter future.

{% csrf_token %}

Impact of Your Donation:

  • Support vocational training for women to achieve financial independence.
  • Provide educational resources and mentorship to children in need.
  • Fund healthcare and wellness programs for mothers and children.
  • Strengthen community initiatives that empower families and preserve cultural heritage.

Akajiugo Charity Foundation is a social-cultural non-governmental organisation dedicated to the support, protection, and welfare of **women and children in Imo State**. We promote care, advocacy, cultural heritage, and community wellbeing.
